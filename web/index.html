GPIO9 also doubles as the BOOT button on the C3 Super Mini. A short press cycles clock faces; hold 2 s to reset WiFi credentials.

First Boot

  1. 1
    Flash the firmware using the button above.
  2. 2
    On first boot the clock creates a WiFi AP called SmartClock-Setup (password: clocksetup). Connect with your phone or laptop.
  3. 3
    A captive portal opens automatically at 192.168.4.1. Select your WiFi network and enter your timezone offset in seconds (e.g. IST = 19800, UTC = 0, EST = -18000).
  4. 4
    The clock connects, syncs time via NTP, and starts displaying. Credentials are saved — it auto-connects on every reboot.
  5. 5
    Press the BOOT button to cycle between Digital, Analog, and Minimal clock faces. Hold 2 s to reset WiFi.
